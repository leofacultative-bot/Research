Metadata Extraction
Extract EXIF, IPTC, and XMP data from the image
Active
▼
What to look forGPS coordinates, camera model, timestamp, editing software, IPTC location fields, and social fingerprints (FBMD = Facebook/Instagram origin).
🔬 JSON Metadata Analyzer Upload or paste metadata JSON ▼
📄
Drop .json file here or click
Supports: exifr, exiftool, jimpl.com, exifmeta.com
○
Run ExifTool on the image
Most complete metadata extractor. Run in terminal:
exiftool -a -u -g1 image.jpg > results.txt
⬇ ExifTool
○
Use an online metadata viewer
No installation needed — upload and view all metadata in browser.
jimpl.com exifmeta.com pic2map.com
○
Decode GPS coordinates if present
Convert DMS to decimal degrees:
Decimal = Degrees + (Minutes/60) + (Seconds/3600) // S and W = negative — e.g. 35°46'12.5"N → 35.7701
📍 Google MapsDMS Converter

Identify social media fingerprints
FBMD in IPTC = Facebook/Instagram origin. Instagram filenames follow USERID_POSTID_HASH_n.jpg.

Reverse Image Search
Find the original source, reposts, and indexed copies
Pending
▼
StrategyRun through all engines — results vary. Yandex is best for structures and faces. Google Lens for landmarks. TinEye for the oldest upload date.
○
Yandex Images — best for buildings & faces
Frequently finds deleted social media posts and original sources.
Yandex Images →
○
Google Lens — best for landmarks
Excellent for famous buildings, venues, tourist spots, and visible signs.
Google Lens →
○
TinEye — find the oldest upload date
Identifies when an image first appeared online.
TinEyeBing Visual
○
Crop & re-search specific elements
Crop just the building, sign, or background. Removing people from frame often yields better matches.
03
Visual Element Analysis
Extract location clues from image content
Pending
▼
ApproachScan the image layer by layer — signs, architecture, vegetation, vehicles, shadows, lighting.
○
Visual Signs & visible text
Street signs, shop names, menus, license plates, logos, language/alphabet. Even partial text is searchable via Google Lens.
○
Visual Architecture style
Facade materials, window design, roof type, balcony style, ornamental patterns.
○
Visual Sun angle & shadow direction
Shadow direction reveals compass bearing. Combined with timestamp, narrows down latitude.
☀ SunCalc.org
○
Visual Mountain or skyline silhouette
Match ridge/skyline against known profiles. PeakVisor simulates the exact view from any point.
🏔 PeakVisorGoogle Earth
○
Visual Vegetation & climate
Palms → tropical. Conifers → temperate/northern. Dry scrub → arid/Mediterranean.
○
Visual Vehicles & infrastructure
License plate shape/color, vehicle models, road side (left/right drive), power line type.
